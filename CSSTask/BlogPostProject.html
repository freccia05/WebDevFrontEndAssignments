>My Thoughts on Code Commenting...

One of my favorite hobbies is CODING!

            
                Sometimes coding comments can be very tedious and annoying... However, they are very important for documentation!
            I once read from a Catholic book, Holiness for Everyone from Eric Sammons on the spirituality of St. Josemaria Escriva.
            He speaks about doing the jobs that no one else notices. Sometimes we become tempted to let simple work slide since it will go unnoticed anyways.
            However, Sammons explains his experience as a computer programmer. Commenting is essential to coding because it might eventually guide
            another programmer making changes to my software. (Holiness for Everyone, Sammons).Commenting not only strengthens your code, 
            but also makes you more of a hard worker. Commenting teaches you that God desires we do everything, whether it is a simple or grand task, 
            be done with enthusiasm and love (Holiness for Everyone, Sammons).
